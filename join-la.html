Los Angeles

Organizing across greater Los Angeles and the Southern Central Coast — anchored by Saticoy Food Hub, the LA Buyers' Club, and the Claremont grain mill brigade.

About

Active in Los Angeles

The Los Angeles chapter organizes across the LA basin and the Southern Central Coast. Saticoy Food Hub serves as the anchor organization for the LA Territorial Market, coordinating the LA Buyers' Club — composed of the LA Tenant Union, Dawah Delivery, and Polo's Pantry — in direct purchasing relationships with campesin@ producers.

The Engineering Brigade is led by 5C Critical Mass from the Claremont Consortium Colleges, working with Saticoy Food Hub to restore a Prohibition-era grain mill. The Inland Empire Buyers' Club is emerging as a connected node in the Southern California network.
