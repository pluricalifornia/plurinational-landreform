Coordinators only: this sheet submits club-level totals. Your club takes orders from its own members first — members should cast individual orders on the member order sheet, which pools them automatically. Then submit your club's combined totals here: box counts plus the item totals your members voted for — these votes are what form the standard box. Quantities from every club are pooled to reach bulk-case pricing, and the price per box emerges from that math: total cost of the collective purchase divided by total boxes. The estimate below uses individual farm prices, so the final invoice is usually lower after bulk savings are applied.
