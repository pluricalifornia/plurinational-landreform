How this works: your order is a vote on what goes into the box. Tell us how many boxes your household wants and vote for the items you want in it. Everyone's votes together decide the box's contents and trigger bulk-case purchases from the farms — and the price per box comes out of that math: total cost of what we collectively buy, divided by total boxes. The more the pool bulks, the lower everyone's box price. You never pay more than individual farm prices. Your coordinator confirms final box contents and price once the round closes. Coordinators: submit club-level totals on the coordinator order sheet instead.

The headline savings are only achieved through bulk — when enough neighbors' orders fill a case, everyone who ordered that item pays the bulk rate.
